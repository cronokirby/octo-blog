you can divide $\alpha_{122}$ by $\alpha_{\bullet22}$ to recover $k_1$.

Both parties know $X_1 = x_1 \cdot G$ and $X_2 = x_2 \cdot G$, as well
as the public key $X = X_1 + X_2$.
